Performance, security, and more

WiMAX (Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access) is a wireless broadband technology that provides high-speed Internet access and data communication over long distances, often used for wide area network connectivity, mobile internet, and wireless backhaul for telecommunications networks.

Performence

WiMAX (Worldwide Interoperability for Microwave Access) offers several advantages for performance in various applications. Here are three reasons to consider using WiMAX

  • Quality of Service (QoS)
  • Non-Line-of-Sight (NLOS) Capabilities
  • High Data Rates
